# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, welcome
constructive technical disagreement, and focus criticism on ideas and code
rather than people. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and abusive
or deliberately disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

## Scope

This standard applies in project repositories, issue trackers, reviews,
discussions, community spaces, and public interactions where a participant is
representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to Sultan Alfaifi through the contact methods
listed in SECURITY.md. Reports will be reviewed in good faith. Maintainers may
edit or remove content, reject contributions, issue warnings, or temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Good-faith criticism, security research, and respectful disagreement are not
violations of this policy.
